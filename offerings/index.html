Our offerings

A multi-sided market for connected care.

KinPet serves three groups: pet owners, pet care providers, and commercial partners. Each group receives different services, and each group pays a different trade-off. This reflects broader processes of platformisation, where services and social activities become organised through shared digital platforms. (Poell et al., 2019)

For pet owners

The whole pet care journey in one place.

HR

Health and routine management

Health tracking, vaccination reminders, diet records, activity logs, and everyday care records make ownership feel calmer and more organised.

BK

Service booking

Owners can book grooming, training, emergency vet access, walking, sitting, and insurance comparisons without moving between separate websites, clinics, and shops.

CM

Community support

Local pet groups, advice posts, reviews, and shared pet stories promise convenience, peace of mind, and a closer relationship with pets.

Convenience depends on continuous data sharing.

Owners give KinPet information about breed, age, diet, health, mood, activity, location, spending habits, and service history. This makes recommendations more personal and helps the platform understand when an owner may need a vet, a groomer, a new toy, an insurance plan, or a premium wellness bundle.

PR

For care providers

Vets, trainers, groomers, walkers, and sitters receive appointment tools, customer discovery, search visibility, service categories, reviews, ratings, profile badges, and paid promotional options.

The trade-off is that visibility is no longer fully theirs. Providers depend on rankings, customer reviews, response speed, and paid promotion.

BR

For commercial partners

Pet food brands, insurance companies, product sellers, and wellness services receive targeted advertising, sponsored recommendations, customer insights, and campaign placement.

The platform does not simply sell pet products. It connects every caring moment with a possible service, subscription, or purchase.
