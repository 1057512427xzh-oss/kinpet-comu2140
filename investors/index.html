Investor Relations

KinPet follows the logic of the lean platform model described by Srnicek (2017). We do not need to own veterinary clinics, grooming shops, training schools, insurance companies, or pet product factories. Our main assets are the interface, the Care Engine, user data, service relationships, and the wider platform ecosystem.

01

Our revenue model is built on several streams. KinPet earns income from premium subscriptions, booking commissions, sponsored recommendations, advertising, insurance partnerships, business analytics, and loyalty programs. Each revenue stream depends on continuing user contribution. Owners contribute pet profiles, health updates, reviews, photos, service histories, and purchase data. Providers contribute labour, availability, ratings, and professional trust. Brands contribute product catalogues, campaign budgets, and sponsored content.

02

KinPet also reflects platformisation because separate pet care activities become organised through one ecosystem (Poell et al., 2019). Vet care, grooming, walking, training, insurance, shopping, and community engagement can all move through KinPet. This makes the platform more useful for owners and more valuable for partners.

03

Following Plantin et al. (2018), KinPet positions itself not only as a service provider but also as an infrastructure for modern pet ownership. As more owners join, more providers and brands have a reason to participate. As more providers and brands join, owners receive more options and more recommendations. These network effects make the platform harder to replace over time.

04

KinPet also competes in the attention economy. In a digital environment where attention is limited, platforms must keep users returning (Webster, 2014). KinPet does this through reminders, alerts, community updates, wellness scores, and personalised care prompts. These tools appear helpful. They also keep pet care inside the platform’s daily rhythm.

The main business advantage is risk outsourcing. Providers remain responsible for service quality. Brands remain responsible for product performance. Owners remain responsible for final care decisions. KinPet coordinates these relationships while focusing on data, algorithms, visibility, and ecosystem growth. In this way, pet care becomes scalable. Love becomes measurable. Connection becomes a platform business. The more owners love, worry, share, and return, the more KinPet grows.
