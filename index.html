KinPet: Reimagining Care Through Connection

About Us

KinPet is a digital platform for people who want to care for their pets in a more connected way. Our keyword is connection. We believe pet care is no longer just about feeding, walking, grooming, or visiting a vet. It is about building a continuous relationship between pets, owners, services, and communities.

About Us

KinPet brings pet owners, veterinarians, groomers, trainers, walkers, insurers, and pet brands into one platform. Through our Care Engine, owners receive health reminders, grooming suggestions, training plans, product recommendations, and local service options. We help owners understand their pets better and make care feel simple, organised, and emotionally meaningful.

The trade-off is small and easy to accept. A stronger bond works better when it can be recorded. The more owners share about their pets’ health, habits, routines, locations, and behaviours, the better KinPet can turn everyday care into personalised connection. We never tell owners that they are not caring enough. We simply help them discover the next service that proves they are.
