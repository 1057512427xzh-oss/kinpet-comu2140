How It Works

KinPet uses a simple interface that feels warm, helpful, and safe. Its affordances include pet profiles, health records, booking buttons, training trackers, reminders, community posts, ratings, and personalised product cards. These features make pet care easy to start and easy to repeat.

01

Step one is to create a Pet Profile. Owners enter their pet’s name, age, breed, health history, diet, behaviour, activity level, and favourite routines. They can also upload photos, medical documents, and location preferences. KinPet presents this as care. At the same time, everyday pet ownership becomes data.

02

Step two is to activate the Care Engine. This algorithm studies appointments, searches, purchases, saved services, health updates, community posts, and user responses. It learns what each pet may need. It also learns when each owner is most likely to worry, click, book, or buy.

03

Step three is to follow the Care Path. KinPet recommends food, training, grooming, vet visits, insurance, toys, and local services. Owners still feel free to choose. But the platform uses choice architecture to make some choices easier to see and easier to trust. Recommended providers appear first. “Most caring choice” packages feel safer. Limited-time wellness offers make action feel responsible.

04

Step four is to join the community. Owners share photos, training wins, reviews, questions, and pet stories. This creates a sense of belonging. It also creates platform value. KinPet relies on participatory culture, where users do not only consume services but also produce content, knowledge, attention, and social value for the platform (Jenkins, 2006).

05

Step five is to stay connected. As owners build records, collect rewards, save providers, and join groups, KinPet becomes harder to leave. This is technological lock-in through memories, health histories, service records, and emotional routines. Leaving the platform no longer feels like deleting an app. It feels like losing a pet care history.
